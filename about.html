Experience Aqua Heaven- The Best of Nature!

Aqua Heaven Fish Farm is an unforgettable experience perched amidst backwaters. Started in 2006, our fish farm is located in South Paravur, Kollam, Kerala.We are one of the leading fish farm in Kollam. We focus on sustainably providing the best tasting, fresh farm raised Peal spot (Karimeen), Tilapia and shrimp.You are always welcome to visit us and buy fresh fish from here. Apart from this we also provide customised packages for farm tourism. The serene and peaceful atmosphere here makes your mind clear and helps you relax in the busy run of your life. Fresh air, live fishing, sizzling atmosphere altogether you will be longing to be here as soon as possible.
